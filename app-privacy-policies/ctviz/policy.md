# Privacy Policy for CTViz

**Effective date:** 2026-03-02  
**Last reviewed:** 2026-03-02 (best-effort; accurate to the team’s knowledge at this timestamp)

CTViz ("the App") is provided by the CTViz developer team ("we", "us").

## Summary
- We do **not** collect, transmit, or sell personal information through our own servers.
- The App processes user-selected files (such as CSV/XLSX imports) **locally on device**.
- We do not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or crash-reporting SDKs in the current app build.

## Data We Process
### Data you provide in-app
- Files you choose to import (for example, CSV and XLSX files).
- Table data you enter manually in the app.

### How this data is used
- Data is used to parse, validate, visualize, and store datasets for app functionality.
- Processing occurs on-device.

## Data Sharing
- We do not share user data with third parties.
- We do not sell user data.

## Data Storage
- App data is stored locally on your device using an on-device SQLite/Room database.
- Imported and manually entered dataset content remains local to your device unless you export, sync, or share it using external tools outside this app.

## Network and Remote Services
- The app manifest does not request Internet/network permissions in the current build.
- The current dependency set does not include Firebase, Ads SDKs, or similar telemetry SDKs.

## Backups and Device Transfer
- Android system backup behavior may copy app data as part of device backup/restore depending on OS settings and platform behavior.
- We do not operate our own backup server for app data.

## Security
- We use reasonable safeguards appropriate for a local-first app design.
- No storage or transmission method is 100% secure.

## Children
- The App is not directed to children under 13.

## Changes to this Policy
- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
- Updated versions will be posted in this policy content and reflected by a new effective date.
